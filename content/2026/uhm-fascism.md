## Intro

In his nominal work [The Ur Fascism](https://theanarchistlibrary.org/library/umberto-eco-ur-fascism) Umberto Eco lists fourteen different principles:

1. Cult of tradition
2. Traditionalism and rejection of modernism
3. Irrationalism and cult of action for action sake
4. Disagreement is Treason
5. Fear of difference
6. Appeal to a frustrated middle class
7. Obsession with a plat
8. Enemies need to be both too weak and too strong at the time
9. Life is a permanent warfare
10. Elitism and Contempt for the weak
11. Everybody is a Hero
12. Machismo
13. Selective populism aka The leader being the voice of the People
14. Newspeak
